building construction

In the construction industry, modern technologies and solutions such as heat recovery from recuperation, solar collectors, photovoltaics, low-temperature materials, as well as remote support systems at home are highly valued. Investors willingly decide on cheap and easy-to-maintain house designs, which are distinguished by a smaller size.

house renovation

The high quality of building materials is also important. The point is that the houses should serve their owners as long as possible and be a place for future generations. A house in an elegant style, high-quality thermal insulation, and the interior of the buildings with stone, brick and marble.

architechture design

Homogeneous and clear patterns were once a hallmark of office architecture, today they are more and more often used in residential buildings as they have become fashionable. Another important aspect is the highest quality materials, thanks to which architects can design buildings that are economical in form, with an impressive appearance.

material supply

Our goal is to provide customers with a complete set of building materials. This means that in our offer they will be able to find everything they will need to implement both individual elements of the project and complete it. The available articles also allow you to carry out any renovation works.

construction consultant

Anyone who starts construction benefits greatly from hiring an advisor to handle construction matters. This solution makes sense because the advisor acts as an intermediary for you and “speaks the construction language.” It is also important to settle disputes that may arise at any stage of construction.

interior design

Do you want to change your apartment, house or small garden? We provide our clients with an experienced team of designers who will listen to your ideas and design a cozy and functional apartment, house or garden for you.

building maintenance

The maintenance of buildings should be understood as maintaining housing estates, houses, etc. in a proper technical condition, while the insufficient technical condition of the building is usually the result of the technical wear and tear of the building or an emergency occurring after the building is put into use.

building renovation

Building renovation is a project that requires much more effort and commitment than a simple property renovation. Conducting any works is related to getting to know the condition of a given facility and thorough knowledge of the architectural trends of the building.
